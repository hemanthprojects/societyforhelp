Mission:

Serving at grassroots through empowerment of deprived children and communities by means of education, health and environmental initiatives to build respect to their dignity, justice, equity and improve quality of life for all.

 

Vision:

A prosperous rural society that is built with positive communities for inclusive and sustainable development of everyone and in every village at grassroots.

 

Aims:

To take up construction activities aimed to help poor for the progress of Agriculture, Health, Nutrition & Family Welfare, HIV/AIDS, Rural & Urban Development & Poverty Alleviation, Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries, Art & Culture, Biotechnology, Children, Civic Issues, Dalit Upliftment, Differently Abled, Disaster Management, Drinking Water, Education & Literacy, Aged / Elderly, Environment & Forests, Food Processing, Housing, Human Rights, Information & Communication Technology, Labour & Employment, Land Resources, Legal Awareness & Aid, Micro Finance (SHGs), Micro Small & Medium Enterprises, Minority Issues, New & Renewable Energy, Prisoner’s Issues, Right to Information & Advocacy, Science & Technology, Scientific & Industrial Research, Sports, Tourism, Tribal Affairs, Vocational Training, Water Resources, Women’s Development & Employment,  Youth Affairs, Economic Standards of Living, Socio-economic Schemes and Any Other.
