Welcome

 

To take up constructive activities aimed to help poor for the progress of Cultural, Moral, Social, Health & Nutrition, Disability, Economic standards of living, Educational conditions of the Rural and Urgan Community by promoting Health and Socio--economic Programme. To provide basic necessities like Food, Water Shelter, Clothing, Medical and Educational Facilities. To take up programmes / activities that will eradicate the evils of Poverty, Unempoloyment, Illiteracy, Economic and Social disparities, Exploitation, etc.,

  

 

Strengthen local capacities of the community on Diaster Management actiities on Pre-diaster, during the disaster and post-disaster situtations and to conduct relief work during natural calamities working to together with government agencies, chartities to bring some relief to the affected area and people.

 

To include and propagate the study and practice of Gandhia values i.e., Truth and Non-Violence (Satya and Kindness to Animas), through action and maintain peaceful, harmonious happy social relations without hatred or violence and also to approach. To lesion with Governement and Non-Government institutions with similar aims and objectives.

To improve the target villages into model villages through integrated activities like Sustainable Agriculture with Non-pesticides Management, Community Development, Improvement of Village Artisan’s Livelihoods, Bio-Energy Management, Social Forestry Development, Fisheries, Animal Husbandry, Extension and Vocational guidance, Women Empowerment, Child Development and care of the Old Aged Persons.
