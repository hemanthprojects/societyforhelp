About Us:

Mr. Ummireddi Jagan Mohan Rao, his friends and associates, all the likeminded people conceived the Society by name Human Empowerment and Livelihoods Promotion (HELP).  The Society was registered with District Registrar, Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, Vizianagaram, Vide Registered No. 389/2007. The Society was registered with its headquarters at Salur, Vizianagaram District, Andhra Pradesh State, India is at the midst of North and South of the District. all the activities through the banner of "Society for Human Empowerment and Livelihood Promotion (HELP)" has been working in India since 2007 and focusing on alleviating poverty and social exclusion. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response. We also focus on generating and sharing knowledge with diverse stakeholders to influence sustainable impact at scale. Our overall goal is the empowerment of women and girls from poor and marginalised communities, leading to improvement in their lives and livelihoods.

Here at Society for Human Empowerment and Livelihood Promotion (HELP) we are driven by a single Goal; to do our part in making the World a better palce for all. Our decision making process is infomred by comprehensive empirical studies and high quality data evaluation. we strive to build productivie relationships and make a positive impact with all of our pursuit.

To take up constructive activities aimed to help Poor and Poorest of the Poor for the progress of Cultural, Moral, Social, Health & Nutrition, Disability, Economic Standards of Living Educational Conditions of the Agency, Rural and Urban Community by Promoting Health and Socio - Economic Schemes.

The whole Objective of the Society is to spend time and efforts by moving though-out the District with the poeople so as to understand various gaps of civic, Welfare, Education, Medical and other income generation connection activiites. The Societymembers felt that by spending theri time with the people the members will be better abridged about the locl necessities, needs and other aspects. Basing on the fair understanding of the gaps, the Society members aimed to bridge the gaps in a phased manner subject to mobilization of the resources men, Material and skill.
