 The Society Registered and do all the activities through the banner of "HELP Society”.

The Society was registered with The District Registrar, Govt of Andhra Pradesh, Stamps & Registrations Department, Vizianagaram, Vide Registration No. 18 of 2021.

Headquarter is located in Plot No. 134, Pydimamba Colony, Kamakshi Nagar, Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh State, India is at the midst of North and South of the District.

The Society has been working in India since 2021 and focusing on alleviating Poverty and Social Exclusion. Wo do all these through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in Health, Education, Livelihood and Disaster Preparedness and response.

Our overall goal is the Empowerment of Women & Girls from poor and marginalized communities, leading to improvement in their lives and livelihoods to do our part in making the World a better place for all.
